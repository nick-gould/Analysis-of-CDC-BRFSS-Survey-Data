# Analysis-of-CDC-BRFSS-Survey-Data

## Objective ##
Build a predictive model for the HeartAttackBool column.

## Dataset ##
Dataset is a CSV file that has been modified from the CDC [2020 BRFSS Survey Data and Documentation](https://www.cdc.gov/brfss/annual_data/annual_2020.html) by the CDC

### Columns ###
1. Gender - Gender
2. Ethnicity - Ethnicity category
3. Location - State of residence
4. Age - Age category
5. Height - Height in meters
6. Weight - Weight in kilograms
7. BMI - Body mass index
8. Sleep - Hours of sleep daily
9. Health - General health condition
10. PoorPhysical - Days during the past 30 days where physical health was poor
11. PoorMental - Days during the past 30 days where mental health was poor
12. LastCheckup - Last routine checkup with a doctor
13. Tetanus - Received tetanus shot in the past 10 years; indicate if vaccine was Tdap
14. TeethRemoved - Number of permanent teeth removed because of tooth decay or gum disease
15. Smoker - Cigarette smoking category
16. ECigarette - Usage of e-cigarettes or other electronic vaping products
17. Diabetes - Have diabetes?
18. Covid - Tested positive for COVID 19
19. ActiveBool - Any physical activity or exercise in the past 30 days
20. AlcoholBool - At least one alcoholic drink in the past 30 days
21. AnginaBool - Have angina or coronary heart disease?
22. ArthritisBool - Have arthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, gout, lupus, or fibromyalgia?
23. AsthmaBool - Have asthma?
24. ChestScanBool - Had a CT or CAT scan of your chest area?
25. CopdBool - Have C.O.P.D., emphysema, or chronic bronchitis?
26. DepressionBool - Have a depressive disorder?
27. DiffConcentratBool - Have serious difficulty concentrating or remembering?
28. DiffDressingBool - Have difficulty dressing?
29. DiffErrandsBool - Have difficulty doing errands alone such as shopping?
30. DiffWalkingBool - Have serious difficulty walking or climbing stairs?
31. FluVaxBool - Had flu vaccine in the past 12 months?
32. HardOfHearingBool - Deaf or have serious difficulty hearing?
33. HeartAttackBool - Had a heart attack, also called a myocardial infarction?
34. HivRiskBool - Injected any non-prescribed drug, treated for STD, or exchanged money/drugs for sex in the past year
35. HivTestedBool - Been tested for HIV?
36. ImpairedVisionBool - Blind or have serious impaired vision, even with glasses
37. KidneyDiseaseBool - Have kidney disease?
38. PneumoVaxBool - Had a pneumococcal vaccine?
39. SkinCancerBool - Have skin cancer that is not melanoma?
40. StrokeBool - Had a stroke?
